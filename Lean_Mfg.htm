What is lean ?

  • Elimination of waste and increase of returns to the company bottom line.

What does Lean mean to the implementer ?

  • Translating customer requirement throughout the manufacturing facility-To pull value
  • Identify and resolve manufacturing issues, almost immediately.
  • Improve manufacturing cost through better control and visibility.
  • Provide all levels of employees to participate in this improvement process, creating trust, morale up build, efficiency, effectiveness, productivity, promote teamwork and finally cost reduction via lower process inventory, less defects, balance production.

7 Wastes (MUDA) of manufacturing:

  1. Overproduction.
  2. Non necessary production.
  3. Excessive Inventory..
  4. Excessive travel.
  5. Excessive waiting.
  6. Workstation non essential movement or not well designed movement.
  7. Yield Issues.

Value Stream Mapping Steps:

  1. Process Mapping activity.
  2. Identify the type of waste, such as value add, non value add and non removal non value steps.
  3. Estimate the waste as cost to company.
  4. Make improvement on as either immediate or a progressive improvement.
  5. Reiterate the process
