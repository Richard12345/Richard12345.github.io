Industrial Engineering

What is Traditional Industrial Engineering ?

  • Method of work study analysis developed over the centuries, but was refine recently during the industrial revolution.

Areas of Industrial Engineering:

  1. Quality Control.
  2. Work measurement analysis.
  3. Production Inventory Control.
  4. Cost Engineering.
  5. Logistics.
  6. Work cell and Line layout design.
  7. Operation management.

Industrial Engineer Attitude: (From Fred. E Meyers, Motion and Time study for Industrial and manufacturing Technologist)

  1. I can reduce the cost of any job.
  2. Cost is number One.
  3. Growth gives meaning and purpose of life.
  4. Problems are opportunities.
  5. I work at 110%, I walk at 125%.
  6. Anything done the same way for more than a year is obsolete.
  7. The person doing the job knows more than I do.
  8. The best ideas are developed in groups
  9. Education is part of my life.
  10. I will be open and honest with everyone.
  11. All my standards and goals are attainable.
  12. I will be patient and understand towards people.
  13. If they do not like me, they do not understand me.
  14. If people do not understand me, I am not communicating.
  15. No one want to hear how good you are, but they want to hear how good they are.
  16. Successful people do what other people dislike to do.
  17. I will not wait on others to deliver the goods.
  18. I will confirm my instructions by asking questions.
  19. I will complete everything I started.
  20. I will say nothing as fact unless I can back it up.
  21. I will never forget who I'm working for.
  22. Never take myself seriously.
  23. I will look and act professional.
