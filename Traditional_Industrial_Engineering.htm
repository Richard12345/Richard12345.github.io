What is Traditional Industrial Engineering ?

  • Method of work study analysis developed over the centuries, but was refined recently during the industrial revolution.

Areas of Industrial Engineering:

  1. Quality Assurance
  2. Quality Control.
  3. Work measurement analysis.
  4. Production Inventory Control.
  5. Cost Engineering.
  6. Logistics.
  7. Work cell and Line layout design.
  8. Operation management.
  9. Test Engineering

Industrial Engineer Attitude: (From Fred. E Meyers, Motion and Time study for Industrial and manufacturing Technologist)

  1. Treat others how you like to be treated
  2. Show Enthusiasm
  3. Show Excellence
  4. Seek for Efficiency
  5. Seek Simplicity in Solution
  6. Seek to learn and to be Effective
  7. Cost and quality is hand in hand.
  8. Growth gives meaning and purpose of life.
  9. Problems are opportunities.Seek opportunities
  10. The person doing the job knows more than I do.
  11. The best ideas are developed in groups
  12. Seek Continuing Education.
  13. Be open and honest with everyone.
  14. Be patient and understand towards people.
  15. No one want to hear how good you are, but they want to hear how good they are.
  16. Do not assume or presume, seek answer by question.
  17. Never take myself seriously.
  18. I will look and act professional.
